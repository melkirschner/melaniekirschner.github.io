About Excel with Mel

This website is all about choosing your type of workout and getting the most out of with the time you can spare during your busy day. With Excel With Mel you can follow along with different workouts and always get your daily sweat in! Whether you are trying to tone your abs or target your entire body we have it all.


After selecting the type of workout you want and the amount of free time you have the workout will begin. It will stay interesting as every minute the exercises will change to easily follow along with and target different muscle groups. Grab your dumbells, mat and jumprope and you're ready to begin. If you don't have any of the equipment feel free to substitue with grabbing a couple of waterbottles or jumping in place. Most importantly, have a great workout!
